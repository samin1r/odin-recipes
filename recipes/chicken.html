Roasted Chicken Recipe

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 1 hrs 15 mins

Additional Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 2 hrs

Servings: 6

Roasted Chicken Ingredients

路 Chicken: This recipe starts with a 3-pound whole chicken. If your bird is larger or smaller, you'll need to adjust the recipe.

路 Seasonings: This roasted chicken is simply seasoned with salt, black pepper, and onion powder.

路 Butter: Butter (or margarine, if you prefer) locks in moisture and gives the seasonings something to adhere to.

路 Celery: Stuff the chicken cavity with celery for subtle vegetal flavor. Plus, the celery will release even more moisture as the chicken cooks.

How to Roast a Chicken Step-By-Step
